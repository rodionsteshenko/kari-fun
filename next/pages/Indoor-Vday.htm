Indoor Vday



Given that Valentine’s Day looks and feels different for all of us, we created a campaign that showcased those unique experiences, while embracing the 2021 reality that this year, we were all under the same “roof.”  Illustrated by Ollie Silvester, the campaign helped make Valentine’s weekend Caviar’s highest volume days ever. And the gift card email alone drove $70k in revenue.
